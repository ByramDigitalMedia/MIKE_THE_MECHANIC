  • Intended to Mean: “I don’t give a d*mn / I don’t care.”

    This is the most likely interpretation. The speaker might be using “nonsense” instead of a stronger word like “d*mn” or “sh*t,” possibly to avoid profanity. So it might mean: “I don’t care at all.”

  • Possible Misuse or Translation Quirk

    • If the speaker is not a native English speaker, they may be trying to say:

    • “I don’t believe in nonsense”
    • “I don’t take nonsense from anyone.”
    • “I don’t tolerate foolishness.”
  • Deliberate Play on Language

    • It could also be intentionally quirky or humorous, like: “I’m not even going to dignify that with a real response.”
